This space features analysis, insights, and reflections from my work in global health policy, pharmaceutical systems, and health reforms. Topics include access to medicines, health systems strengthening, regulatory policy, and lessons from comparative health systems research.

Lessons from 30 Years of Health Benefits Package Evolution in Colombia

Coming Soon Health Systems

Colombia's journey from explicit health benefits packages to implicit universal coverage offers valuable lessons for other countries pursuing universal health coverage. This analysis explores key success factors and persistent challenges in defining what should be covered by public health systems.

Universal Health Coverage Colombia Health Policy

The Challenge of Vaccine Equity: Reflections on COVID-19 Distribution in Latin America

Coming Soon Vaccine Access

The COVID-19 pandemic exposed deep inequities in vaccine access across Latin America. Drawing from research with Oxfam International, this piece examines the structural factors that contributed to these disparities and what they mean for future pandemic preparedness.

COVID-19 Vaccine Equity Latin America

Why Generics and Biosimilars Matter for Health System Sustainability

Coming Soon Pharmaceutical Policy

As health systems face increasing pressure from rising pharmaceutical costs, policies promoting generics and biosimilars are more important than ever. This analysis examines effective policy approaches across Latin America and their impact on both access and sustainability.

Generics Biosimilars Sustainability

Strengthening Local Drug Manufacturing: Opportunities and Challenges

Coming Soon Manufacturing

Local pharmaceutical manufacturing can enhance supply chain resilience and improve access to medicines. Based on work with ECLAC, this piece explores strategies to strengthen local manufacturing capacity in Latin America, including improved access to active pharmaceutical ingredients.

Manufacturing Supply Chain Pharmaceutical Industry

The Role of Digital Technologies in Communicating Public Health Information

Coming Soon Digital Health

Digital technologies offer unprecedented opportunities to communicate complex public health information to diverse audiences. This piece explores effective strategies for using data visualization, social media, and digital platforms to enhance health literacy and inform policy debates.
